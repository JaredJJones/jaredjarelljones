Technical Solutions to Abstract Problems About Jared

Born in California, but raised in Northwest Florida, Jared has always been naturally inquisitive when it came to technology. What began and a general fascination, quickly became a "how does this work" attitude. Jared excels in taking abstract probelms and finding the best logical solution to them. As a professional web developer, this logic is always challenged when creating solutions for clients, regardless of what they may be. Extensive experience in the realm of digital marketing allows him to use technical knowledge to solve problems that don't necessarily fit the schema of logical means.

In his free time, Jared enjoys walking his dog, playing video and tabletop games, figuring out new recipes in the kitchen, working out, and even just relaxing. This in addition to his work attitude gives him a great deal of balance and an all around positive demeanor that is contagious to many of those around him.
